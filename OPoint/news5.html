Saudi Aramco Awards Larsen & Toubro $1.5B Contract For CCS Project
Carbon Herald | 24 February 2025
 
 

Saudi Aramco has contracted Larsen & Toubro, specifically its LTEH arm, for a $1.5 billion project centered on carbon capture and storage. This effort directly supports Saudi Arabia's 2060 net-zero ambition and the interim target of 44 million tons of CO2 sequestration by 2035.

Transition to renewables taking longer than thought, senior BP figure tells MPs
London Evening Standard (UK) | 26 February 2025
 
 

The transition to renewables is taking longer than previously thought, a senior member of BP has said, on the same day her company announced it would slash funding for clean energy and ramp up fossil fuel extraction. Louise Kingham, who leads BP in Europe, told the Scottish Affairs Committee there were still "great economic opportunities" in the North Sea.
